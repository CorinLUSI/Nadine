Our Philosophy

Back then in Frankfurt, Grandma Helga magically created delicious jellies, cakes, cookies, and several other treats - all of them with that unforgettable taste that brought you back to the golden days.

Being brought up by my grandparents I was allowed to collect those wonderful experiences as a child and still love remembering and dreaming about those amazing times.

I created this company to share with you my one-of-a-kind collection of old German delights, which is a tribute of my love for the recipes of my childhood.
